Housing Infomation

Ozone Park is mainly a residential area. It is a blue collar community where people tend to remain there their whole lives. Everyone knows each others, and there would be severak generations of families thriving there. Housing options range in different prices, contributing to the economic diversity of the community. Houses are primarily pre-war single or multi-family homes. There are also several Tudor-Style homes. Overall, residents have reported that it is a fairly safe place to live and raise a family.
