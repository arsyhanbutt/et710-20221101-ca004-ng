Fun Activities to do in/near Ozone Park

PRO LINE ARCHERY LANES

  • RATING: 4.5

Pro Archery Lanes has been around since the mid 70s. It had been originally organized as a place for archers to get together and enjoy a game of archery. Overtime, it has grown into a family oriented business that is open to the general public.

This particular location is one of the most popular places to visit for a fun activity in Ozone Park. Vistitors describe the place as safe, suitable for all levels and ages, and with friendly staff.


Location:
95-11 101st Ave
Fl 2
Ozone Park, NY 11416

For more information visit: prolinearchery.com

QUEENS ZOO

  • RATING: 4

If you are looking for a fun, yet educational place to learn about wildlife and nature then the Queens Zoo is perfect for you. It is an 18-acre zoo with 75+ species of animals and plants. There are several programs for differnt groups. This is includes but is not limited to: Aldult Programs, Youth and Young Adults, and Children and Family Programs.

This particular location is one of the most popular places to visit for a fun activity in Ozone Park, especially if you want to entertain children. Vistitors describe this place as entertaining and educational.


Location:
5351 11th St
Corona, NY 11368

For more information visit: queenszoo.com

LASER BOUNCE

  • RATING: 4

One of the most popular places to visit for some family fun is Laser Bounce. There are plenty of games and courses in this place for a day filled with fun. It even hosts parties that are fun for kids of all ages.

Visitors describe this place as ideal for parties, with friendly and attentive staff.


Location:
80-28
Glendale, NY 11385

For more information visit: laserbounce.com
